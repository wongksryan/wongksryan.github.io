Putty Launcher — Version 1

Adiabatic Calculations

Derived closed-form energy and pressure relationships using adiabatic expansion models to predict muzzle velocity and chamber efficiency, then validated calculations using an experimental setup.

CAD + Simulation

Modeled full assembly and validated mechanical integrity under operating loads. Iterated on barrel geometry and chamber size to improve consistency and reliability.

Final Product Showcase

Fully functional launcher capable of repeatable launches of 15 gram putty slugs at 50m/s muzzle velocity given a maximum pressure of 50psi. Optimized valve timing, pressure control, and projectile-tube fitting for reliable operation; launches were done on secured private property in Hong Kong as to alleviate legal concerns.
