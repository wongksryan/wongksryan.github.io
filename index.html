About Me

Hey! I'm Ryan (Kai Sum) Wong. I'm currently studying to become a Mechanical Engineer, but I have a general curiosity about all kinds of engineering.

I enjoy learning how the world works and applying said knowledge for fun. For example, learning about adiabatic expansion, then combining it with efficiency and work calculations to see how fast I could launch a chunk of putty given a certain air pressure.

My main interests in mechanical engineering are Aerospace and Medical Technologies, as my projects demonstrate. I'm also a big fan of seals(the animal).
